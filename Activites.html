Social activity

This activity includes trips, camps, group recreational activities, parties and recreational competitions. It also includes many competitions such as: chess - the ideal student competition and the ideal student competition - field visits and organizing seminars and conferences for students.

Artistic activity

It aims to develop hobbies and highlight students, artistic talents, such as individual and group singing, individual and group playing, theatrical acting and plastic arts. Most universities, especially Cairo, Ain Shams and Helwan universities, support this activity through their own budget.

Sport activitie

Universities provide gyms equipped with the latest sports equipment and green sports fields prepared for the practice of various sports. Universities implement this activity by organizing competitions in football and all other sports and games for all faculties of the university among students and faculty members to spread the spirit of cooperation among them.

Cultural activitie

Cultural activity provides many opportunities for students to freely express their views on community issues and participate in solving the problems they face, as well as providing the opportunity for creative students in various fields of literature; To show their talents in poetry, fiction writing and other literary arts. Universities implement many writing competitions to highlight the literary talent among distinguished students.

Student Activities

Student activities work on consolidating and documenting human ties between students and faculty members, and one of the most important ways for students to integrate with society, whether inside the university or rehabilitate them for society abroad. Contribute to the entertainment of students during the study period and help in acquiring many skills.
