Quality guarantee unit


unit vision

Raising the position of the Faculty of Computers and Information - Fayoum University in order to be an accredited entity capable of achieving competitiveness at the local, regional and international levels.


massage of unit

The unit seeks to spread the culture of quality to improve the educational system and the research and service activities provided by the college in light of the standards of the National Authority for Quality Assurance of Education, in order to gain the confidence of the beneficiaries in the outcomes of the educational process in order to achieve competitiveness locally, regionally and internationally.


Definition of accreditation

Recognition granted to an institution, if it can prove that its programs comply with the declared and approved standards and that it has systems in place to ensure quality and continuous improvement of its academic activities, in accordance with the declared controls determined by the accreditation body.


Definition of accreditation

It is the means to ensure the achievement of institutional capacity, educational effectiveness, and the application of academic standards, which derive from the mission of the concerned institution, which have been defined and achieved in accordance with the corresponding standards, whether nationally or internationally. And that the level of quality of learning opportunities, research and community participation is considered appropriate and meets the expectations of different types beneficiaries.


What if it is not approved ?

If it is not accredited, all graduates will face the following problems



  1. Weak job opportunities in the local, regional and international market compared to graduates of accredited colleges.

  2. A graduate of a non-accredited college should be able to prepare any academic equivalency for an international college or even a regional college. And if he desires the equivalency, he will have to prepare it through another accredited college.

  3. The college lost its independent structure and joined the name, administration, and knowledge of an accredited College of Computers and Information.

The importance of unity


Evaluation is one of the most important episodes of the educational system, and it is the most intertwined element with all components of the educational process, including (teacher), (learner) and (curriculum).

Without the evaluation, and its approved standards and specifications, the application of the principle of justice and equal opportunities will be absent, between graduates of the same college, and even between graduates of Egyptian universities each other.

Therefore, work was to formulate evaluation standards for all parties to the educational process, to update, develop, and keep pace with all current technological developments. Hence the importance of this unit, which works to improve the level of university graduates, who have sufficient ability to meet the requirements of the local and international labor market, which depends on higher skills for learning.

The importance of this unit: is due to its support by:



  1. Developing methods and means of evaluation and using modern methods of evaluation.

  2. Achieving speed, accuracy, transparency and fairness in assessment, examinations, follow-up and statistics work.

  3. Contribute to the achievement of the identity of the university.

  4. Emphasis on the dominance of electronic integrated management that would achieve the quality of the teaching and learning environment at the university

  5. Strengthening e-learning.

  6. Working on building structured question banks that reflect the targeted educational outcomes and measure the extent to which the academic standards of the programs are achieved.

  7. Contribute to the unification of the standards and specifications of examinations for students of the same specialization in different universities in order to achieve justice among graduates of Egyptian universities and corresponding colleges.

  8. Achieving justice among students of one major in corresponding colleges.

  9. Correcting the course of the teaching and learning processes to achieve quality.

  10. Linking the study program to the requirements of the labor market.

The Disaster Crisis Management Unit at the Faculty of Computers and Information, Fayoum University, aims to provide a stable and safe work environment by preparing and implementing insurance plans and programs and dealing with expected crises in the work environment

The aim of unit


The unit's strategic objective

The Alumni Follow-up Unit aims at continuous and effective communication with the graduates of the college, to meet the needs of the graduates during their professional life, and to develop them in line with the needs of the labor market and the service of the surrounding community, in a way that achieves the mission and goals of the college.


Procedural goals

The goal of the Graduate Follow-up Unit is to help the graduate to :
Communicate with the college and with all former and future colleagues and feel a sense of belonging.
Helps his colleagues to find suitable job opportunities for them.
Provides the college with practical experience to ensure the college's communication with the labor market and the external community.
Obtain specialized training and participate in seminars held by the college. Graduates learn about the college’s news and the latest developments in it
